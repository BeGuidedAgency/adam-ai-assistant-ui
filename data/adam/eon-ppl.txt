4. CORE FUNCTIONS OF PPL
------------------------------------------------------------

4.1 Needs Mapping  
Individuals express needs: food, housing, energy, mobility, care.  
Creates a real-time map of unmet needs.

4.2 Contribution Ledger  
People log contributions: skills, time, care, labour, expertise.  
Makes invisible value visible.

4.3 Access System  
Goods and services are shared, loaned, or accessed — not owned.  
Aligns with EON’s “Strategic Access” model.

4.4 Trust Network  
Reputation is built through contribution and reliability.  
This replaces transactional systems with relational ones.

4.5 Community Coordination  
Groups can:
- form teams
- manage local projects
- coordinate resources
- make decisions transparently
